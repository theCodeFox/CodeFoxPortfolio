About Me

Hello there! I started my journey into tech after my previous career, training croupiers. I fancied a change, a job that would challenge me, puzzle me and frustrate me. A new career where I could bring joy, continuously learn and not start a shift at midnight. It wasn't much after that I discovered the joy of coding. I quickly jumped feet first into Northcoders (a Manchester based coding bootcamp), knowing that all I needed was a foot in the door and my own curiosity, determination and focus would aid me in my data quest.

So, why data?

It is a rapidly changing industry where the solution is derived by detective work, current expertise and newly aquired knowledge. It allows me to continuously grow, while covering many areas and truly feeding my curious mind. We produce so much data in our everyday lives, it's a waste to not utilise it and allow for smarter, faster decisions. Decisions not driven by the musings of an individual but of the group. This approach however does have it's drawbacks, the largest risk, for me, being re-enforcing a groups bias. It is our responsibility as data developers, analysts and users to recognise this and build it out. As much as we can, anyway!

Fail fast, fail often, fail forward.
